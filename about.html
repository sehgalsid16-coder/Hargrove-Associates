The Founding of Hargrove & Associates

James R. Hargrove founded this firm after nearly a decade at one of Los Angeles' most prestigious litigation practices. The reason was simple: at large firms, the clients with the most complex, most consequential matters are often served by the most junior attorneys. He believed — and still believes — that the people with the most at stake deserve the most experienced counsel.

Hargrove & Associates opened its doors in downtown Los Angeles with a deliberate mandate: accept fewer cases, invest more deeply in each one, and never stop fighting until the client's objective is reached. That mandate has not changed.
